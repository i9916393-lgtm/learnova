Intelligent Matching

The Pairing Algorithm

How does LearnNova find the perfect match? Here's the logic behind the magic.

Step 1 — Data

Every student's offered skills and wanted skills are stored in structured data. The system holds a complete campus skill inventory.

Step 2 — Compare

Your "I want to learn" is matched against other students' "I can teach" — and vice versa. Mutual interest = perfect match.

Step 3 — Connect

Only 100% aligned matches are shown. No random suggestions — every result is a genuine complementary fit.
